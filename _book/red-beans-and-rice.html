Chapter 5 Red beans and rice

5.1 Ingredients

Garlic Red onion Celery* Capsicum Red kidney beans (1 can) Onion powder Salt Pepper Chicken stock (2 cup; tbc) White rice (2 cup) Butter (20g)

5.2 Steps

  1. Brown onion and garlic. Add celery and capsicum. Cook until tender.
  2. Add kidney beans and spices.
  3. Cook 12 mins on rice setting (low pressure) (tbc). Release pressure naturally.
