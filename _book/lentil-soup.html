Chapter 2 Lentil Soup

2.1 Ingredients

Carrot Onion Celery Garlic Butter Oil 250g Lentils 400g Tomatoes 1L Water Stock cubes Parsley Pepper

2.2 Steps

  1. Heat butter and oil. Add onion celery and carrot. Cook until onion transparent
  2. Add lentils + tomatoes, water stock cubes. Cover and bring to boil.
  3. Simmer 1.5h until lentils ready.
